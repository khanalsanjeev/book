Reality is an emergent function of three interacting vectors. It begins with Observation, the immediate data of present moment acting as initiation mechanism. This is woven together by Memory, the temporal thread that provides depth and context to the sequence of existence. Finally, Pattern Analysis allows for the recognition of recurring geometries within the flux of experience.

This book introduces a simple but powerful idea:
Reality emerges through awareness.
